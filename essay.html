Quick Tips

Plan first: spend 3–5 minutes outlining ideas; one idea per paragraph.

2

Use variety: mix simple and complex sentences; aim for precise vocabulary.

Band descriptors (short)

Band 9

Fully addresses the task with fluent, natural language. Highly accurate grammar and sophisticated vocabulary. Cohesion flawless.

Band 7–8

Answers the task well, clear progression of ideas, occasional errors that don't impede communication; good range of vocabulary.

Band 6

Generally effective but limited flexibility; noticeable inaccuracies in grammar and vocabulary; ideas sometimes unclear.

Band 5 or below

Insufficiently addresses the task; frequent breakdowns in coherence and control of grammar and vocabulary.

Common checklist
  • Clear thesis statement
  • 2–4 supporting paragraphs
  • Varied sentence structures
  • Accurate punctuation & spelling
