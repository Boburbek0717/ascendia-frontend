Band descriptors (short)

Band 9

Fully addresses the task; fluent, accurate grammar; sophisticated vocabulary; cohesive and natural.

Band 7–8

Addresses task well; logical progression; occasional errors that don't impede meaning.

Band 6

Generally effective but with noticeable inaccuracies; limited flexibility in expression.

Band 5 or below

Insufficient task response; frequent coherence and grammar breakdowns.
